Our Mission

Leave no one behind. Our mission to give the leaders of tomorrow the tools they need to empower their student community, aiming to create real change in areas including but not limited with the scope of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. Youth will change the world!

Let us acknowledge and celebrate what youth can do to build a safer, more just world. Let us strengthen our efforts to include young people in policies, programmes and decision-making processes that benefit their futures and ours.

Ban Ki-moon
